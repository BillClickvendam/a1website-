About us

Founded over a decade ago, A1 Media Production Group has grown from a modest studio in Broadhurst Taung, Gaborone, to a leading force in media production and artist management. Our journey began as AeroSpace Studios, driven by the vision and passion of our founding members. Today, we are proud to be a premier destination for creative excellence in the industry.

Our Team

A1 Media Production Group is comprised of a team of industry professionals who are as passionate about their craft as they are skilled. From producers to engineers, our team works tirelessly to ensure that every project we undertake meets the highest standards of quality and creativity.

Our Mission 

At A1 Media Production Group, our mission is simple: to bring your creative visions to life. We specialize in a range of services, including music production, artist management, and promotional campaigns. We believe in the power of collaboration and are dedicated to providing unparalleled support to our artists and clients.

Our Vision 

To be a global leader in media production, known for our innovative approach and commitment to excellence. We strive to push the boundaries of creativity, delivering results that not only meet but exceed the expectations of our clients.

Our Legacy

The legacy of our late co-founder, Native Flex, remains a cornerstone of our company. His visionary leadership and dedication to nurturing talent have left an indelible mark on A1 Media Production Group. We honor his memory by continuing to uphold the values he instilled in us and by fostering the next generation of creative talents.

Native Flex

Remembering Native Flex: A Legacy that Shaped A1 Media Production Group Native Flex wasn't just a music producer and engineer—he was a visionary and a cherished friend who played a pivotal role in the early days of A1 Media Production Group. Over a decade ago, when our company was still known as AeroSpace Studios in Broadhurst Taung, Gaborone, Native Flex's genius and passion for music were key to our growth and transformation.



Native Flex's incredible journey in the music industry began with his formation of the group Tribe Soul alongside his close friend and protégé, DJ Enton. As a mentor, Native Flex guided DJ Enton, sharing his vast knowledge and shaping his talent. Tribe Soul was more than just a musical duo; it was a testament to their deep friendship and shared vision.



In time, Native Flex's influence helped expand the team by bringing another prodigious talent into the fold—Ma_EAzzy. This trio, united by their love for music and driven by Native Flex's inspirational leadership, was instrumental in realizing the vision for what would become A1 Media Production Group. Their collaboration marked a new era, transitioning from AeroSpace Studios to a powerhouse in media production.



Native Flex's legacy is woven into the very fabric of A1 Media. His contributions went beyond just production; he instilled a culture of excellence, creativity, and camaraderie. Even as we mourn his absence, we celebrate his enduring impact on our company and the music industry in Botswana.



The lessons he imparted, the music he crafted, and the relationships he built continue to inspire us. Native Flex's spirit lives on through the work we do at A1 Media Production Group, as we strive to honor his memory by achieving greater heights and nurturing new talents, just as he did. His story is a cornerstone of our history and a guiding light for our future.  

AeroSpace Studios

From AeroSpace to A1 Media The transition from AeroSpace Studios to A1 Media Production Group marked the evolution of a company dedicated to excellence in media production and artist management. The foundation built by AeroSpace Studios has allowed A1 Media to grow into a premier destination for creative services, offering a wide range of solutions from music production to promotional campaigns.

Looking Forward Today, A1 Media Production Group stands as a beacon of creative excellence, constantly evolving and innovating while staying true to the values instilled by AeroSpace Studios. The journey from a small studio in Broadhurst Taung to a leader in the media production industry is a testament to the power of vision, dedication, and collaboration.

Dato Seiko 

In the vibrant tapestry of Botswana’s music scene, Dato Seiko stands out as a mesmerizing figure. Her enchanting presence and soulful voice have captivated hearts and minds, earning her a place among the most compelling RnB talents of our time.

A Mesmerizing Presence Dato Seiko’s allure is undeniable. With striking features that echo the mythic beauty of ancient Medusa, she exudes a mystique that draws listeners in. Her aesthetic, reminiscent of 90s icons like Aaliyah, combines a sense of nostalgia with contemporary flair. This unique blend sets her apart, creating a persona that is both timeless and refreshing.

Soulful Lyrics and Captivating Melodies Her music is a journey through emotion and experience, each note and lyric crafted with precision and passion. One of her standout lines, “have you come across souls with not enough soul,” resonates deeply, reflecting her introspective and poignant songwriting. It’s this lyrical depth that makes Dato Seiko not just a singer, but a storyteller who weaves her life and thoughts into every track.

Roots in Botswana, Aiming for the Stars Dato Seiko’s journey began in Botswana, a nation with a rich and diverse musical heritage. Her rise in the RnB genre is a testament to her talent and dedication. She carries herself with the poise and confidence of a seasoned artist, yet maintains the raw, authentic energy that first drew her audience in. It’s this duality that makes her performances unforgettable.



An Ode to the 90s with a Modern Twist Her music carries the essence of 90s RnB—a golden era of emotive ballads and smooth rhythms—yet it’s infused with her personal touch. This blend of old and new creates a sound that is both familiar and innovative, appealing to a broad spectrum of listeners. Dato Seiko’s ability to navigate these two worlds seamlessly speaks to her versatility and artistry.
